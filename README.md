# Hi there! 👋

I am **Ivan Moriá Borges**, a Brazilian Music Therapist with a Master's in **Neurosciences**, and a **PhD Candidate** currently serving as a **Professor at UFMG** (2025–2027).

As a multi-instrumentalist and researcher, I work at the intersection of **Music Therapy**, **Neurosciences**, and **Computational Music Analysis**. My work focus on **Music Information Retrieval (MIR)** and the development of digital tools for **micro-analysis** and **clinical assessment** in music therapy. By leveraging **automated data extraction** and **MIDI analysis**, I explore how technology can enhance our understanding of social cognition and optimize therapeutic processes.

---

### 🔬 Research & Academic Background
* 🎓 **Substitute Professor in Music Therapy (UFMG, 2025–2027):** Teaching **Ear Training**, **Harmony**, and **Improvisation** for undergraduate music therapy students.
* 🎓 **PhD Candidate in Music (UFMG):** Developing digital tools in **Python**, **MAX/MSP**, and **JavaScript** for MIDI data processing and statistical analysis, focused on **Digital Music Therapy Assessment** and open-access clinical microanalysis.
* 🧠 **MSc in Neurosciences (UFMG):** Researched social cognition in music therapy and interactive musical improvisation.
* 📋 **Academic Contributions:** Peer reviewer for *Music & Science* (UK), member of the Research Commission at the Brazilian Union of Music Therapy Associations (UBAM).
